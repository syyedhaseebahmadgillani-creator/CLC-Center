A quick look at our journey

We combine compliance, empathic agent training, and measurable processes to drive results for ACA, Medicare, and final expense campaigns.

2005–2010 — Foundation

Started with a focused team of agents for life & health insurance outreach; built early partnerships with independent brokers and regional agencies.

2011–2015 — Growth & Compliance

Scaled training, added HIPAA processes, expanded to serve U.S. states with targeted ACA enrollment programs and performance reporting.

2016–2020 — Digital & Performance

Adopted CRM and cloud dialing; introduced quality monitoring, QA scoring, and weekly KPI dashboards for partners—improving conversion and retention.

2021–Present — Remote Excellence

Operates internationally with bilingual agents; offers 24/7 support and data-driven optimization for ACA, Medicare, and final expense campaigns.
