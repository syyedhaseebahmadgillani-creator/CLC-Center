A Quick Look at Our Journey

We combine compliance, empathic agent training, and measurable processes to drive results for ACA, Medicare, and final expense campaigns.

2005 — Our Beginning

Multidials Solution was founded with a small, focused team serving life and health insurance campaigns. Early partnerships with independent brokers laid the foundation for future growth.

2010 — Expansion

We expanded our agent team and introduced structured training programs, enabling high-quality ACA enrollment campaigns across multiple U.S. states.

2015 — Compliance & Growth

HIPAA-compliant processes were implemented. The company grew its client base nationally, enhancing reporting and lead management systems.

2020 — Digital Transformation

CRM integration, cloud dialing, and KPI dashboards improved conversion rates. Quality monitoring and regular client feedback became standard.

2025 — International Excellence

Our international remote team now provides 24/7 support with multilingual agents, serving ACA, Medicare, and final expense campaigns worldwide.
