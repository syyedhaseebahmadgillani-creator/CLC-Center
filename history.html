Our Record of Excellence

Since 2005, Care Line Call Center has been a trusted partner in American insurance campaigns, helping agencies grow while supporting clients nationwide with professionalism and care.

2005–2010: The Foundation Years

Care Line Call Center began operations with a small team of 10 agents, focusing on life and health insurance lead generation. Within five years, our commitment to quality service built long-term partnerships with multiple U.S. insurance providers.

2011–2015: Expanding Across America

Our company grew to 100+ trained agents. We expanded into ACA (Affordable Care Act) enrollments and began serving clients from Florida, Texas, and California. We built a reputation for consistent performance and compliance excellence.

2016–2020: Digital Innovation

By adopting advanced CRM systems and cloud-based dialing, Care Line Call Center became a fully integrated remote team — allowing 24/7 operations across multiple time zones for major insurance campaigns across the U.S.

2021–Present: Excellence and Growth

We now operate with international offices and serve multiple U.S. insurance partners, offering bilingual support and customer-first call handling. Our team continues to deliver exceptional ACA enrollments, final expense, and Medicare support services.
