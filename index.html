About Care Line

Established in 2005, Care Line Call Center has completed over 2,000 projects across healthcare and senior-care campaigns. We combine experienced call agents, campaign specialists, and compliance-first processes to deliver predictable outcomes for health and final expense campaigns.

Our strengths include empathy-centered conversations, fast onboarding, strict data security, and measurable results. We pride ourselves on high client satisfaction, transparent reporting, 24/7 support options, and flexible, affordable campaign packages tailored to each client.
